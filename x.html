Mirror

A short fictional story based on a personal dream

I had looked far too long,
longer than I'd ever had before,
too long to just neglect what presents itself before me.

A glace turned into a glare,
a glare to a stare,
a reflection of me.

An unideal self,
and the picture enriched an underlying rage within me,
and surfaced a reaction I could not stand to resist at the moment.

I striked the reflection.

Just a crack,
spitting the image in two.
separating the old perspective into two new.

This new reflection brought a slight ease to my mind,
but it was not enough.

I struck again,
new shards,
new perspectives.

Till pieces of the mirror shattered off the wall,
and the once perfectly circular flat mirror which once before gave such sharp portrait of oneself
now gave a scattered and and shattered shape,

one of a heart.

My actions were a destructive relief that created something beautiful.

A lens of love and acceptance of imperfection.

Give the way you view yourself a new light.
