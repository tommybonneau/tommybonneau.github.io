Who am I?

A mortal man above anything else,
but a man who aspires to be the best version of himself,
and to have life worth living with a cause worth dying for.

To be the loving person
that those in my life deserve.

To be a civic servant,
where my purpose is to benefit my community,
and my obligation to do the right thing.

To uplift my community and it's members
in a way a samaritan should extends a hand to their neighbor in need.

To be a life-long scholar,
and a renassiance man,
in an effort to master my human nature,
and bring clarity into the world.

And finally,
to create tangible works,
so my thoughts and ideas may live on indefinently.
